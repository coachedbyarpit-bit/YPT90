Introduction

CoachedByArpit ("we", "our", "us"), operated by Arpit Kushwaha, is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information — name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide when booking a call or enquiring about our services

How We Use Your Information

Our website uses Google Tag Manager and Meta Pixel to collect anonymous usage data for analytics and advertising purposes. These technologies may place cookies on your device. By using our website, you consent to this data collection.

You can opt out of Meta's ad tracking at facebook.com/ads/preferences and manage Google tracking at myaccount.google.com.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and accessed only by authorised personnel. Health information shared during coaching is treated with strict confidentiality and used solely for delivering your program. We retain your data for as long as necessary to deliver our services and comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at teamcoachedbyarpit@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.
