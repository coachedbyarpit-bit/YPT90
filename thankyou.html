Before the call

What To Expect

  • 01
    Check your email right now You'll have a calendar invite with the call link. Add it to your calendar so you don't miss it. Check spam if you don't see it within 5 minutes.
  • 02
    Pull out your last blood report if you have one Not mandatory — but if you have a recent blood test, have it handy. We'll go through what the numbers mean for your fat loss and energy specifically.
  • 03
    Think about your last 3 attempts What did you try, how long did it last, and why did it stop? This gives Arpit the context to tell you exactly where the system broke — not your willpower.

    Show up on time, in a quiet spot This is 45 minutes built entirely around you. Not a group call. Not a recorded webinar. Arpit will be fully present — come ready to be honest about where you are.

    You will leave with clarity — not a pitch By the end of the call you'll know exactly what's wrong, why it's been happening, and what the next 90 days should look like. Whether you join YPT90 or not, that clarity is yours to keep.
