I was a lawyer before I became a fitness coach. BA LLB from Nirma University. I was good at law. But I kept coming back to one question — why do smart, disciplined people fail at fitness?

The answer wasn't willpower. It was systems. Or rather, the complete absence of a system built for their actual life. Every program I saw was designed for someone with unlimited time, no family, and no stress. That person doesn't exist.

So I built YPT90. A 3-phase transformation protocol designed specifically for busy working parents 30–55. Not based on bro science. Based on blood reports, hormone panels, gut health, sleep architecture, and 2000+ real client transformations.

How I think about this

The Philosophy

🔬

Science Over Bro Science

Every protocol is referenced to research. No guesswork, no trends. If it can't be explained by physiology, it's not in the program.

🏗️

Systems Over Willpower

Willpower is finite. Systems aren't. The goal is to make healthy defaults so automatic that discipline becomes irrelevant.

🩸

Data Over Guessing

Blood reports don't lie. We track biomarkers, not just the scale. Testosterone, thyroid, insulin resistance — the things that actually determine your results.

👨‍👧

Life-First, Not Fitness-First

Your family, your career, your social life — these don't pause for your fitness. The program works around them, not against them.
